Table MARD - Storage Location Data for Material
SYNTHETIC example (abap_wiki template) - data dictionary extract.

Field        DataType    Len.   Key     Description
-----------  ----------  -----  ------  --------------------------------------------
MANDT        CLNT        3      X       Client
MATNR        CHAR        18     X       Material number
WERKS        CHAR        4      X       Plant
LGORT        CHAR        4      X       Storage location
LVORM        CHAR        1              Deletion flag at storage-location level
LABST        QUAN        13             Unrestricted-use stock (valuated)
UMLME        QUAN        13             Stock in transfer (storage location to storage location)
INSME        QUAN        13             Stock in quality inspection
EINME        QUAN        13             Total blocked goods-receipt stock
SPEME        QUAN        13             Blocked stock
RETME        QUAN        13             Blocked returns stock
VMLAB        QUAN        13             Unrestricted-use stock of the previous period
VMUML        QUAN        13             Stock in transfer of the previous period
KZILL        QUAN        13             Stock in quality inspection (inventory adjustment)
DLINL        DATS        8              Date of last inventory posting
PLIFZ        DEC         3              Planned delivery time in days

Functional notes:
- The stock available for the reallocation calculation is LABST (valuated
  unrestricted-use stock). The other stock types (UMLME, INSME, SPEME) are NOT available.
- Logical key: MATNR + WERKS + LGORT. A material has one MARD row per storage location.
- For the minimum stock see MARC-MINBE (plant level, not storage location).
