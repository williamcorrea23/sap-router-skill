The whole skill

One picture: knowledge in, trust out.

Two ideas run the skill. Knowledge decides where an answer comes from — a higher source wins. Trust decides how much the tool acts on its own. Between them sits a fixed pipeline: deterministic where it's safe, Claude only on the judgment calls, a human gating anything that changes the system. Afterwards the human works the findings as a worklist and signs off — and every review teaches the skill (a fixed gap becomes a regression-locked test).
