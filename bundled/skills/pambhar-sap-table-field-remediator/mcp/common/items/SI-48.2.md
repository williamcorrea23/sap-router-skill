## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Solution

## Description

The DIMP Enhancements Actual Labour Costing / Time Recording functionality is not available with SAP S/4HANA.

No further developments are planned in this area.

## How to determine relevancy

Following objects are not available with SAP S/4HANA.

| Transactions not available with SAP S/4HANA   | ADAA   |
|-----------------------------------------------|--------|

| Programs/Reports not available with SAP S/4HANA   | AD02PRCE - Program to compute actual labor pricing AD02ACTV - Activity allocation conversion AD02_HIERARCHY_DETERMINE_PSJ - Project based payment run - get project hierarchy   |
|---------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|

## Required and Recommended Action(s)

No influence on business processes expected.

Eliminate the indicated usages from your customer objects.
