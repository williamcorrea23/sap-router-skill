| Business Impact |       2267331 | S4TWL - Project System ITS Services |

## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Solution

## Description

The following ITS services in Project System are not available any longer in S/4HANA as of SAP S/4HANA 1511:

CNW1             WWW: Confirmation

CNW4             Project Documents

SR10              Claims

## Business Process related information

Selected ITS services for document search, confirmations and creation of claims are not available as of SAP S/4HANA 1511.

## Required and Recommended Action(s)

Knowledge Transfer to key- and end user.
