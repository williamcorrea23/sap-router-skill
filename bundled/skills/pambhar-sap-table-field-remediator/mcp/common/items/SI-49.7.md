## Symptom

You plan to manage returnable packages with either your vendors or your customers. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Reason and Prerequisites

Returnable Packaging Logistics (IS-A-RL) is not the target solution in SAP S/4HANA and therefore deprecated. Even if it's not yet defined when Returnable Packaging Logistics will become obsolete and be replaced, which means it's still in maintenance until communicated otherwise, but will receive no new features and innovations. SAP recommends implementing the new industry cloud solution SAP Returnable Packaging Management as the strategic next-generation solution.

## Solution

SAP Returnable Packaging Management (LO-RL) is an industry cloud solution helping companies to manage the logistics of reusable returnable packaging materials in a supply chain. Reusable packaging materials such as containers, pallets, tanks, and load carriers are used to move products safely and efficiently between business partners. With this product, a packaging manager can determine and plan the exact quantity and location of these reusable materials in the supply chain, thereby minimizing loss, waste, and rentalcost of these packaging materials.

Being an Industry cloud solution SAP Returnable Packaging Management is aligned with SAP's strategy and has continuous investment with a well-defined roadmap

SAP Returnable Packaging Management integrates with S/4 HANA back-end systems, S/4HANA Cloud and S/4HANA OP. For prerequisites, please refer to chapter 'Relevancy'.

## Business Process related information

## 1. Packaging Logistics

When packaged goods are shipped to a customer or vendor the decrease of the package is automatically captured in a packaging account. When the packages are returned, the increase is posted to the same packaging account. The status of serial-numbered packages can be defined, changed, and tracked individually. Stock in plant is displayed based on storage-location and stock-type. Statements for packaging-accounts showing the movements for a certain time-period can either be created or received and matched.

## 2. Packaging Planning and Collaboration

Packaging demand can be forecasted based analysis of the historical consumption pattern. Account-levels below reorder-point can be replenished automatically via the creation of a purchase-order in S/4HANA.

## 3. Returnable Packaging Rentals

The amount for the rental of packages can be calculated for serialized packages and bulk packages.

For more details, refer to the product assistance of SAP Returnable Packaging Management - Link

## Required and Recommended Action(s)

New functionality and enhancements are planned for Industry cloud application SAP Returnable Packaging Management to the SAP roadmap. Returnable Packaging Logistics (IS-A-RL) in SAP S/4HANA will be in maintenance until communicated otherwise.

SAP Returnable Packaging Management features included in the previous and  planned for any future release can be found in the Roadmap Explorer.

It is recommended that new SAP customer implementations start with Industry cloud application SAP Returnable Packaging Management.
