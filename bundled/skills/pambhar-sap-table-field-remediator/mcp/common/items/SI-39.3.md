## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA. In this scenario, the following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item applies.

## Solution

## Description

Sales Support: Computer-Aided Selling (SD-CAS) - (see SAP Help LINK) is not available in SAP S/4HANA, because it is not the target architecture. We recommended that you use SAP CRM on-premise (side-by-side with SAP S/4HANA) or SAP Cloud for Customer.

## Business Process-Related Information

Sales Support is no longer available in SAP S/4HANA. The creation of new business documents or editing of migrated business documents is not possible.
