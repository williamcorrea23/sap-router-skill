## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA, on-premise edition. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Solution

## Description

Product Mobile Asset Management (MAM) functionality is not available with S/4HANA. For your mobility related requirement to manage maintenance management, please refer SAP roadmap.

With this change, we plan to discontinue the Objects mentioned in the piece list and delete these objects from SAP S/4HANA 2021 FPS1 onwards. Refer note 2250183 for further details.

## Business Process related information

No influence on business processes expected.

## Required and Recommended Action(s)

Please check if you use any of the objects mentioned in the piece list in any of your Custom Applications , reports etc  they will not work anymore and you have to create alternative arrangement.
