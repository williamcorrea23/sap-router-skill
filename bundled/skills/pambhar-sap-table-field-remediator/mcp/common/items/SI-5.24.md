## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA 1809 FPS0, on-premise edition. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Solution

With SAP S/4HANA, on-premise edition 1809, the integration of Global Trade Management (GTM) with Commodity Management processes (not ACM!) is partially supported.

Pricing and Payement Events integration in Commodity Management is now supported in GTM. This functionaility is encapsulated within the Business Function ISR_GLT_CMMINTEG.

NOTE: For functionalities that existed earlier in business function ISR_GLT_CMMINTEG, please refer to notes 2551942 and 2671010
