## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Reason and Prerequisites

Reason: Conversion from SAP ERP for Discrete Industries & Mill Products (ECCDIMP) to SAP S/4HANA.

## Solution

## Description

The automotive industry solution in SAP ERP Discrete Industries & Mill Products (ECCDIMP) for tracking of inbound deliveries and shipments is not available with SAP S/4HANA.

To calculate and track the exact time of arrival, the recipient of goods could use the tracking of inbound deliveries and shipments. Event information could be received via EDI message with type TRXSTA and processed with reference to an inbound delivery or inbound shipment. The event location had to be defined as node of a route assigned to the delivery or shipment document. The event time is used then to calculate the expected time of arrival based on the duration information stored with the route for scheduling.

The automotive industry solution for tracking of inbound deliveries and shipments has a low customer adoption rate. With SAP Transportation Management, a much more comprehensive and scalable solution exists.

## Business Process related information

The industry-specific solution for tracking of inbound deliveries and shipments is not available with SAP S/4HANA.

## How to Determine Relevancy

The Simplification Item is relevant, if:

EDI messages of message type TRXSTA have been processed (check for entries with MESTYP = 'TRXSTA' in table EDIDC with transaction SE16)

## Required and Recommended Action(s)

Evaluate SAP Transportation Management to manage and track shipments and related deliveries before conversion to SAP S/4HANA.

Archive EDI messages of message type TRXSTA before conversion to SAP S/4HANA.
