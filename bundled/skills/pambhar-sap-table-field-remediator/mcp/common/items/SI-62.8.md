## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion or an upgrade to SAP S/4HANA 1909 or higher. You are using functionality of SAP IDE (Intercompany Data Exchange) for deregulated utilities markets. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Solution

## Description

The concept of non-billable services on level of points of delivery (PoD) is comprehensively

described: https://help.sap.com/viewer/905da66b76d1449fbe6ac26484842994/1909.000/ en-US/8b5fce53118d4308e10000000a174cb4.html

The maintenaince (creation and change) of non-billable services is supported via the

supply scenario and supply scenario

generator: https://help.sap.com/viewer/905da66b76d1449fbe6ac26484842994/1909.000/e n-US/ff60ce53118d4308e10000000a174cb4.html Non-billable services on PoD level are maintained automatically in the background in the context of start and end of supply and change of supplier processes. Display is available on PoD level (transaction EEDM11 "Display point of delivery", Following these concepts of SAP IDE, transactions for maintaining individual nonbillable services are not required. Following transactions are therefore deprecated in SAP S/4HANA:

Transaction EPODSRVC1 ("Create PoD service")

Transaction EPODSRVC2 ("Change PoD service")

Transaction EPODSRVC3 ("Display PoD service")

These transactions have been delivered before the concept of the supply scenario has been provided. Successor of these transactions is the functionality of supply scenario generator.

This note is only relevant for you if you are using non-billable services in your actual system, i.e. there are entries in table ESERVICE.

## Business Process related information

Use the functionality of supply scenario should be applied.
