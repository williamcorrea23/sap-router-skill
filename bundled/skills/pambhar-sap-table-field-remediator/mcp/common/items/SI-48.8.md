| Business Impact |       2270839 | S4TWL - Enhancements subcontracting special stock types not available |

Symptom You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Solution

## Description

DIMP Enhancements Subcontracting special stock types "SC RTP" (I), "SC Vendor consignment" (J), "Customer Stock" (B), "SCCustomerOrderStock" (F) are not available within SAP S/4HANA 1511.

## Business Process related information

No influence on business processes expected

## Required and Recommended Action(s)

Knowledge Transfer to the end user about the non availability of the special stocks in SAP S/4HANA 1511.

"Customer Stock" (B) and "SCCustomerOrderStock" (F) will be available from SAP S/4HANA 1610 onwards.
