## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA, on-premise edition. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Solution

## Description

Transaction CIC0 provides a basic Call Center help desk application which is technically outdated and not available within SAP S/4HANA.

## Business Process related information

Business processes that require a help desk / call center integration cannot be executed in the customer interaction center any more. Instead they have to be integrated into the interaction center / help desk applications offered by SAP S/4HANA, SAP CRM or SAP C/4HANA.

Transaction not available in SAP S/4HANA on-premise edition 1511 CIC0

## Required and Recommended Action(s)

Business processes that require a help desk / call center integration should be integrated into the interaction center / help desk applications offered by SAP S/4HANA, SAP CRM or SAP C/4HANA.
