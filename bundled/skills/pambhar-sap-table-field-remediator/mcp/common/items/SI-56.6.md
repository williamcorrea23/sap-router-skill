| Business Impact |       3519685 | S4TWL - Business Function /ACCGO/BF_FP01 is removed from S/4 HANA 2025 SP 00 release |

## Symptom

Customer has planned a system upgrade to S/4 HANA 2025 FPS 00 or any later release and Business Function /ACCGO/BF_FP01 is switched on in current release (any release before S/4 HANA 2025 FPS 00).

## Reason and Prerequisites

## Business Value & Description

Business Function /ACCGO/BF_FP01 is no longer needed and would be deleted in release S/4 HANA 2025 FPS 00 and all future releases.

## Solution

## Business Process related information

Business Function /ACCGO/BF_FP01 contains switches for Prepayment processes and integration of Agricultural Contract Management with Prepayment processes.

## Required and Recommended Actions

If in the current release the business function is switched on with an upgrade planned to S/4 HANA 2025 SP 00 or higher, please take care of the below mentioned actions before upgrade. If correct actions not taken, it would lead to data loss.

## Based on the product requirement

If the Customer wishes to use ACM and Prepayments it should be ensured that the Business Funtions /ACCGO/BF_ECC & BUSINESS_FN_PPMG should be active and switched on.

If the Customer wishes to use ACM only it should be ensured that the Business Funtions /ACCGO/BF_ECC should be active and switched on.

If the Customer wishes to use Prepayments only it should be ensured that the Business Funtions BUSINESS_FN_PPMG should be active and switched on.

If the Customer wishes not to use either of the products ACM & Prepayments there is no need to activate and switch on any specific Business Functions.

## How to Determine Relevancy

The conditions that identify relevancy are mentioned below.

A system upgrade is planned to S/4 HANA 2025 SP 00 or higher.

In the current release the business function /ACCGO/BF_FP01 is switched on.
