| Business Impact |       2810717 | S4TWL - FSSR: Correction index 1 (ISSRFLDEO and ISSRFLDEC) |

Symptom You execute a system conversion in SAP S/4HANA, On Premise edition. The following SAP S/4HANA transition worklist entry is relevant in this case.

## Reason and Prerequisites

A change of Index-1 for ISSRFLDEO and ISSRFLDEC to unique is required.

## Solution

## Description

For the tables mentioned above, there is an index that is not unique. This may lead to duplicate entries that cause terminations in the application.

## Required and recommended action(s)

Proceed as described in SAP Note 2771381.
