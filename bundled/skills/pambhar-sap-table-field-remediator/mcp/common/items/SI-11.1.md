| Business Impact |       3087756 | Deprecation of Cash Operations for China (COPC) and EPIC Embedded Cash Budgeting |

## Symptom

You would like to use Cash Operations for China (also known as COPC) to manage your cash pools and cash concentration, or you would like to use EPIC embeded Cash Budgeting functions. However, this is not recommended since the solution of COPC is out of development and will be deprecated in SAP S/4HANA 2023.

## Reason and Prerequisites

The functions provided by Cash Operations for China have been replaced by the solution Cash and Liquidity Management under component FIN-FSCM-CLM.

The functions provided by EPIC embedded Cash Budgeting have been replaced by Cash Budgeting in SAP S/4HANA Finance for cash management, version for China.

## Solution

In order to leverage the latest product features and maintainance delivered by SAP, please make sure you use Cash and Liquidity Management for your treasury management requirements. If you would like to use Fiori-based apps, you are recommended to use SAP S/4HANA 1809 or above.

Please be reminded of the risks of using COPC:

Feature and/or processing logic explanation,  suggestions about system operation and/or configuration, and how-to requests are not supported via customer incidents. You can find further details in SAP note 83020.

COPC folder under "SAP Menu -> Accounting -> Financial Accounting -> Banks -> Country Specifics -> China -> Cash Operations" will be officially removed and use of T-codes will not be authorized after one release.

## Other Terms

EPIC, COPC, Cash Concentration, Cash Pool, EPIC embedded Cash Budgeting
