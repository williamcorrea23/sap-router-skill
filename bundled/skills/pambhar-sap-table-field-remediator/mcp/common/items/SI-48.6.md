## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Solution

## Description

The DIMP A&D Flight Scheduling solution functionality is not available with SAP S/4HANA.

A&D Flight Scheduling solution was developed, but never released for productive use for customers.

## Business Process related information

No influence on business processes expected - no productive use for customer.

## Required and Recommended Action(s)

No actions required from the customer side.
