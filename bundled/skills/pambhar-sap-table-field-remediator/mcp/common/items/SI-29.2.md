## Symptom

The predictive scenarios PREDICTDAMAGECODE and PREDICTMAINTOBJECTPART are unavailable without replacement with SAP S/4HANA 2021 onwards  and SAP S/4HANA Cloud 2302.

This affects the 'Predicted Maintenance Object part' and 'Predicted Damage code' in the Malfunction data section in the details page of the app Perform Maintenance Jobs (F5104A).

## Reason and Prerequisites

Functionality is obsolete.

## Solution

Functionality is obsolete.
