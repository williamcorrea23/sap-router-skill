## Symptom

If you are upgrading system from SAP ERP to SAP S/4HANA and you have Business Place names consumed in your self-developed programs, the names might be empty after the upgrade.

## Reason and Prerequisites

This is a system upgrade information.

Previously, Business Place names were stored in the field J_1BBRANCH-NAME . To enable the language-dependent names of Business Place, from SAP S/4HANA 2020, text table J_1BBRANCHT is added to store both new and modified Business Place names. SAP standard functionalities were adjusted to support this change. However, if Business Place names are selected from the field J_1BBRANCH-NAME , in some cases, the names displayed in your self-developed programs are empty.

## Solution

CDS view P_BUSINESSPLACE is released to cover different cases of Business Place names for both legacy and new data. To ensure that Business Place names can be displayed in your self-developed programs, please use the CDS View as the data source instead of J_1BBRANCH in your custom program.
