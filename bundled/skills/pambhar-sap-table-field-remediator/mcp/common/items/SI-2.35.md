## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Solution

## Description

Along with SAP R/3 Enterprise Edition 4.6C for some R/3 modules Data Modeler (SD11) content was delivered by SAP. Later on this was no longer updated or complemented with additional data models. Hence this content gradually became outdated during SAP R/3 Enterprise Edition 4.7, SAP ERP 5.0 and SAP ERP 6.0 timeframe. The meanwhile obsolete SD11 content has been removed in SAP S/4HANA. There are currently no plans to deliver new content for SD11.

## Business Process related information

No impact on business processes is expected.

## Required and Recommended Action(s)

Inform your users who did access this SD11 content in the past about this change.
