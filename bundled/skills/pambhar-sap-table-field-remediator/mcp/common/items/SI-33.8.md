## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA, on-premise edition. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Solution

## Description

The add-on SAP Product and REACH Compliance (SPRC) will not be supported within SAP S/4HANA.

SPRC supports process and discrete industry manufacturers in meeting the respective directives and regulations. Both need to prove the safety of substances, products, and their uses according to the REACH (Registration, Evaluation and Authorization of Chemicals) chemical regulation. Discrete industry manufacturers need to provide proof of compliance with environmental directives, such as EU RoHS, China RoHS, JIG, and GADSL.

Product Compliance management of SAP Environment, Health and Safety Management Extension (EHSM) provides compliance functionality for the needs of the discrete industry. If you use the discrete industry solution of SPRC or Compliance for Products (CfP), you are able to migrate to SAP EHSM Product and REACH Compliance .

For the process industry solution of SPRC, no direct successor exists.

The focus of SPRC version 2.0 was to support process and discrete industry manufacturers to become REACH compliant by using functionalities, such as campaign management for supply chain communication, bulk material assessment and registration and endpoint management. With the deadline for the last REACH registration period in 2018, the focus of customers will switch towards functionality which supports them to remain REACH compliant.

Process industry customers can use SAP EHS Management Product Safety as follows:

Use Safety Data Sheet authoring and distribution to communicate Use and Exposure scenarios of your products to your customers

Manage Substance registrations with authorities including registration numbers and volume thresholds Use Substance Volume Tracking capabilities to track and monitor the volumes of substances in your products.

Discrete industry customers can use SAP EHS Management Product and REACH Compliance as follows:

Maintain and monitor the compliance status of your raw materials for each supplier

Aggregate and monitor the compliance status for your products based on the supplier information.

The provision of new capabilities, which will cover both process and discrete industries requirements, is planned to be available as part of SAP S/4HANA for product compliance and SAP S/4HANA Cloud for product compliance . Selected functionality to manage and ensure the marketability of your products is already available as of SAP S/4HANA 1808 / 1809. Please check https://www.sap.com/products/product-compliance.html for additional details.

## Business Process related information

No influence on business processes expected

The tools of SPRC version 2.0 will only be relevant for process industry manufacturers until the deadline of the last REACH registration period in 2018. The focus of customers will then switch to functionality which supports them to remain REACH compliant.

All transaction codes which were delivered with the SPRC add-on are not available within SAP S/4HANA.

## Required and Recommended Action(s)

You need to uninstall the add-on before you do a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA. Follow SAP Note 2298456 to perform the uninstallation.

If you use the discrete industry solution of SPRC or CFP, you are able to migrate to Product and REACH Compliance of SAP Environment, Health and Safety Management (EHSM), which provides functionalities for discrete industries. For details refer to http://help.sap.com/ehs-comp or SAP Note 1489703.
