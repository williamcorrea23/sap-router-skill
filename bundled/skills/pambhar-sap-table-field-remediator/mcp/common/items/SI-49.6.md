## Symptom

You plan to use Sales Scheduling agreement with Delivery orders (TAM). The following business impact note is relevant for you.

## Reason and Prerequisites

Sales Scheduling agreement with Delivery orders (TAM) is not strategic, and customers should evaluate the next-generation Just-in-Time (JIT) Supply to Customer solution in SAP S/4HANA

Sales Scheduling agreement with Delivery orders (TAM) is a deprecated component. This means that it is still supported but will be replaced by the next-generation Just-inTime (JIT) Supply to Customer solution in SAP S/4HANA in the long term. The date is not set yet.

## Solution

Scheduling Agreement with delivery orders (TAM) is a special type of Scheduling agreement. The process flow is Sales Scheduling agreement ->Sales order (TAM Delivery order) -> Delivery ->Invoice.

This process came into existence when Just in Time (JIT) solution was not available. Since next-generation Just-in-Time (JIT) Supply to Customer solution is now part of the standard solution, it is recommended to use that solution with summarized JIT calls instead of TAM.

## Business Process related information

'JIT Supply to Customer' is the S/4HANA successor of the SAP ERP 'JIT Inbound' solution. For customers using JIT processes it is recommended to use 'JIT Supply to Customer'from S/4HANA 2021 onwards as 'JIT Inbound' will also be deprecated. For details please refer to note - https://launchpad.support.sap.com/#/notes/3159740

JIT Supply to Customer supports the following key features:

Receive EDI messages to create and update customer JIT calls

Usage of sales scheduling agreements for JIT processing

Outbound delivery creation based on customer JIT calls

Monitoring of customer JIT calls

Stock availability analysis

Demand analysis to compare forecast delivery schedules with customer JIT calls

JIT delivery schedule creation based on customer JIT calls for improved material requirements planning

Action framework, defining

allowed actions per internal processing status

change of internal processing status by action execution

action to be executed based on a change of the external status

interlinked actions, combining multiple actions to be processed together subsequently.

Extensibility options for processing JIT calls through EDI message

Definition of custom-specific actions

Extending SAP standard actions in pre-processing and post-processing
