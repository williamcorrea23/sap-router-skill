## Symptom

You are doing a system conversion to SAP S/4HANA, on-premise edition. The following SAP S/4HANA Transition Worklist item is applicable in this case.

## Solution

## Description

The iView was as an option for Pharmaceutical Sales Reps who were running CRM Mobile Sales to manage their inventory of free samples. This functionality is not available within SAP S/4HANA. No functional equivalent is avialble.

## Business Process related information

Business Processes using this solution will no longer work. An alternative solution has to be developed in a customer project.

## Required and Recommended Action(s)

Customers using the solution have to develop their own UI on their platform of choice to be deployed to mobile devices of their sales reps.

## How to Determine Relevancy

The iView was meant to be used to provide pharmaceutical sales reps with the ability to review their stock of drug samples (van stock). It provides a view into the standard ERP inventory.

This note is relevant for customers usign the SAP Portal and have configured this iView.
